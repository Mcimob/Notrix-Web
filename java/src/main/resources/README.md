# Welcome to Notrix Web
Notrix Web allows you to visualize many competitions from Kaggle. Furthermore, you can also add your own competitions/solution sets to analyze. If you have only a singular solution you want to compare with other solutions of a competition you can do that too!

To get started you can simply click on a competition either in the visualization or in the list. This will take you to a visualization of all viable solutions to that competition.

## Own Solutions
All of your uploaded solutions are available to you under the "_Saved_" tab. Whenever you upload a new solution or solution set, you will be notified when the analysis is complete. 

You can upload either whole solution sets (shown as competitions) or single notebooks to any existing competition. 
When you upload a new competition, the title of the competition will be the name of the uploaded zip-file. You can also include a `README.md` in the competition zip, which will act as the competitions description.
